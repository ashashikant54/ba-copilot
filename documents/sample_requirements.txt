Project: Employee Onboarding Portal

Business Problem:
Our company currently onboards new employees using email and paper forms.
This process takes 2 weeks and involves HR, IT, and Finance teams manually
processing each new hire. Errors occur frequently and new employees often
start without proper system access.

Current Pain Points:
- New hire paperwork takes 3-5 days to process manually
- IT access provisioning is delayed by an average of 4 days
- HR spends 40% of their time on repetitive onboarding tasks
- No visibility into where each new hire is in the process
- Compliance documents are sometimes missed or filed incorrectly

Business Goals:
- Reduce onboarding time from 2 weeks to 2 days
- Automate IT access provisioning on day 1
- Give HR real-time visibility into onboarding status
- Ensure 100% compliance document completion
- Reduce HR manual effort by 60%

Stakeholders:
- HR Director: Wants faster onboarding and less manual work
- IT Manager: Wants automated access provisioning
- Finance: Wants accurate cost tracking per new hire
- New Employees: Want a smooth first-day experience

Users:
- HR Team (10 people) - will manage and monitor onboarding
- New Employees (50 per month) - will complete their own onboarding
- Managers (30 people) - will approve and track their new hires
